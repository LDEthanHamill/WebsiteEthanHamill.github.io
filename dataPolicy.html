Data Policy



The Data Controller is Ethan Hamill. You can contact me using any contact details you hold for me, or using the contact form below.
This policy was last updated on Wednesday, 23 October 2019.

Data I collect

I collect names, emails + email addresses + Phone & Mobile Numbers + Occasionally postial/residential addresses.

What I use your data for

Your data is used to allow me to perform work for you and communicate with you and others about that work.
The lawful basis of the data collection is the contract or contracts I have with you, or the work I need to do to prepare
any quotes or estimates. I will only use and collect postal addresses for invoicing, contracting and preparing estimates.


Who I share your data with

I share your data with other organisations as a direct part of providing you with my services. I don't use your contact
information for marketing or share it with other third parties without asking.
Some of the organisations I share your data with include Google, Dropbox, Apple, Squarespace and my accountant. Some of these
organisations are based outside the AU. These companies have customers in the AUS and are subject to the same data protection
laws as me. Sometimes I might need to provide your information to a third party as required by AU law, or if I change a service provider.

I may ask you for your consent to recommend you for work to a third party. I will explicitly ask for your consent before providing your contact details and you can choose what I provide. I will never provide your data without asking.

Public sharing of your name

I may share your name publicly on my website to credit your work on a project. I will never share any information other
than your name. This information is collected and shared as part of my legitimate interest to market my work and credit you for yours.
If you don’t wish me to use your name to credit your work, you may object to my legitimate interests by contacting me.

How long I keep your data

Data is retained for the length of the project, 5 years from the last contact we’ve had or as long as required to maintain business records.

Your rights

You have the right to:
– Be informed about how I use your data
– Have access to your data
– Correct information I hold about you
– Ask for information I hold about you to be deleted or it’s use to be limited. I can’t delete your information
if keeping it is required to be kept under AU law.


Data Security

Security of your information is important. I do what I can to secure your information. Some services (like email)
are not secure once they have left my computer. However, I do what I can to improve the security of your data.
I use the following:
– A VPN to encrypt traffic on public or shared WIFI connections
– Encrypted Drives
– Remote wiping of portable devices if lost
– Strong passwords and factor-two authentication wherever possible.
